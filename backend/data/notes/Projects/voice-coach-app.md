# AI Voice Coach App

## Concept
AI-powered singing coach that listens to users sing and provides real-time feedback and training.

## Key Features
- **AI Voice Assistant** - Listens to user singing via microphone
- **Real-time Feedback** - Immediate coaching on pitch, tone, breathing, technique
- **Structured Roadmap** - Progression path from complete beginner to competent singer
- **Expert Training** - Trained on best voice coaching knowledge and techniques
- **Personalized Training** - Adapts to user's voice type, range, and progress

## Target Market
- People with no singing ability who want to learn
- Casual singers wanting to improve their technique
- People who can't afford or access traditional voice lessons

## Value Proposition
Turn someone from zero singing ability to being able to sing well through AI-guided training with personalized feedback.

## Technical Considerations
- Audio processing and pitch detection
- Real-time voice analysis
- AI model for coaching feedback
- Structured lesson curriculum
- Progress tracking and gamification
