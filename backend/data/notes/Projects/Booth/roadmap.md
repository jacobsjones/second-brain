# Booth Roadmap

## Vision
**Airbnb for music studios** — connecting artists with recording studios for short-term hire.

## Phase 1: MVP (Months 1-2)
### Goal: Validate demand with minimal features

**Core Features:**
- [ ] Studio owner onboarding (basic profile, photos, pricing)
- [ ] Artist booking flow (search, view, book, pay)
- [ ] Simple availability calendar
- [ ] Stripe payments (hold until booking confirmed)
- [ ] Email notifications (booking confirmed, reminder, review request)

**Tech Stack:**
- Next.js 14 (App Router)
- PostgreSQL (Neon or Supabase)
- Prisma ORM
- NextAuth.js (Google OAuth)
- Stripe Checkout
- Vercel hosting
- AWS S3 (image storage)

**Success Metrics:**
- 10+ studios listed
- 5+ bookings completed
- Net Promoter Score > 7

---

## Phase 2: Growth (Months 3-4)
### Goal: Improve conversion and retention

**Features:**
- [ ] Advanced search (equipment filters, location radius, price range)
- [ ] Studio reviews & ratings
- [ ] Instant booking vs. request-to-book options
- [ ] Messaging between artist and studio owner
- [ ] Mobile-responsive design polish
- [ ] Cancellation policies (flexible, moderate, strict)

**Growth Hacks:**
- [ ] Referral program (£20 credit for referrer and new user)
- [ ] Instagram integration (studios can auto-post availability)
- [ ] Student discount verification

**Success Metrics:**
- 50+ studios listed
- 30+ bookings/month
- 20% month-over-month growth

---

## Phase 3: Scale (Months 5-6)
### Goal: Market leader in UK, prepare for expansion

**Features:**
- [ ] Mobile apps (React Native)
- [ ] Insurance integration (equipment damage protection)
- [ ] Subscription model for studios (featured listings, analytics)
- [ ] Dynamic pricing (surge pricing for peak times)
- [ ] Group bookings (band rehearsals, workshops)
- [ ] Equipment rental add-on

**Markets:**
- [ ] London (launch)
- [ ] Manchester
- [ ] Birmingham
- [ ] Glasgow

**Success Metrics:**
- 200+ studios
- 150+ bookings/month
- £10k monthly revenue
- Break-even achieved

---

## Phase 4: Platform (Months 7-12)
### Goal: Ecosystem for music creators

**Features:**
- [ ] Booth Pro (subscription for frequent artists)
- [ ] Studio management tools (CRM, invoicing, tax reports)
- [ ] Marketplace for session musicians
- [ ] Integration with DAWs (export stems directly)
- [ ] AI matching (suggest studios based on genre/equipment needs)

**Expansion:**
- [ ] Europe (Berlin, Amsterdam, Paris)
- [ ] US (NYC, LA, Nashville)

## Risk Mitigation

**Risk: Studios don't join**
- Mitigation: Offer 3 months 0% commission, free professional photography

**Risk: Artists don't book**
- Mitigation: Guarantee first booking discount, partner with music schools

**Risk: Quality issues (damaged equipment, no-shows)**
- Mitigation: Insurance integration, security deposits, verified user badges
